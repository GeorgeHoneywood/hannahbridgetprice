What student doesn't love pesto pasta?
&nbsp;

&nbsp;
### Ingredients
* 100ml oil
* 145 sun-dried tomatoes
* 40g pine nuts
* Handful of basil
* 4 tbsp nutritional yeast
* Salt to taste
&nbsp;

&nbsp;
### Method
1. Blend it all together and serve with pasta or in a sandwich!
